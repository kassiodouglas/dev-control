Panel Component

The Panel Component provides a slide-in panel that can be opened from the left or right side of the screen.

Usage



  
Panel Title

  
This is the content of the panel.

Inputs

  • id (string): A unique identifier for the panel. Required.
  • size ('xs' | 'sm' | 'md' | 'lg' | 'xl' | 'xxl' | 'full'): The width of the panel. Defaults to 'md'.
  • position ('left' | 'right'): The side from which the panel appears. Defaults to 'left'.
